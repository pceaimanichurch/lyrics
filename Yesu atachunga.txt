Jesu atachunga,​
Mchunga wetu,​
Naye atafuta machozi yetu;​
Mkononi mwake ​
Hatuna hofu,​
Daima twapata ​
Kwake wokofu. 

​Jesu atachunga tumemjua,​
Na sauti yake ​
Twaitambua;​
Naye akitwonya​
Ni kwa upole,​
Tu kondoo zake​
Hata milele.

Jesu atachunga;​
Aliwafia ​
Kondoo wakubwa​
Na wana pia;​
Kwamba awajua,​
Kondoo zake,​
Hutiwa alama​
Ya damu yake.​

​Jesu atuchunga;​
Mikono yake,​
Imewaambata​
Kondoo zake;​
Hapati wadhuru ​
Adui yule​
Jesu awalinda​
Hata milele.​
