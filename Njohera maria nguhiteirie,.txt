Mwene hinya ningukuinira, 
Na ngoro hamwe na meciria, 
Ningwambararia ritwa riaku, 
Erohimu we mwene muoyo. 

Njohera maria nguhiteirie,
Hari we ndingiuga ndiehetie,
Niwe unjohagira mehia,
Ona ukahonia ndwari ciothe.

Ngoro yakwa nigukugoca,
Gwata kiria gi thiini wakwa, 
Ningugaatha ritwa riamure,
Tondu ni ritwa rina uhoti.

Njohera maria nguhiteirie,
Hari we ndingiuga ndiehetie,
Niwe unjohagira mehia,
Ona ukahonia ndwari ciothe.

Wakurire muoyo uyu wakwa,
Kuri irima nene cia gikuu,
Waturaga unjuthiriirie,
Ngoima mbirira hotanite.

Njohera maria nguhiteirie,
Hari we ndingiuga ndiehetie,
Niwe unjohagira mehia,
Ona ukahonia ndwari ciothe.

Uthuthuragia ngoro ciandu,
Ukamenya meciria mao,
Gicuthiririe ngoro yakwa,
Undathimire maria mega.

Njohera maria nguhiteirie,
Hari we ndingiuga ndiehetie,
Niwe unjohagira mehia,
Ona ukahonia ndwari ciothe.

We niwe wathaga ruhuho,
Na mai riua ona riera,
Hotithia nie ndungata yaku,
Ndure ngukenagia ithe witu.

Njohera maria nguhiteirie,
Hari we ndingiuga ndiehetie,
Niwe unjohagira mehia,
Ona ukahonia ndwari ciothe.
